About Bible Cave

Bible Cave exists to present basic Bible truths in this day and age of "relatism", where absolutes do not seem to exist anymore in our society, where there is no black and white, but all is "grey", no "right" or "wrong", whatever seems right to a person is right. The only problem with these ideas are the facts that culture does not change truth, neither do popular opinions, ever!

We aim to present the truth of the Bible as it is in Jesus, as well as expose the works of darkness.
