History Of The Christian Church

History will outline what has happened during the time since Jesus was upon the earth, and how the church has strayed so far from the Christian gospel message of Jesus Christ.

How was the church spiritually in Christ's day? I put it to you it was corrupt. The Pharisees and Sadducees were constantly in-fighting, and showing the general public how "holy" they were. Jesus did not think much of they declared holiness, though.

Is the church today any better? According to the Bible we are living in the "end times", and God describes the last day church as "Laodecian", or luke warm - a very negative description given by Jesus.
