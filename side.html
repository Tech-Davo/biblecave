Side Issues

Here we expose various doctrines that have crept into Christianity, which seem to be mostly taking the focus of Christians off Christ and following Him, to having "pet" theories that people are making out to be "salvational" issues, when they are really not.

This page is still under construction, but here we will be discussing doctrinal theories such as the "names of God", "we need to keep the feasts now", and "the Holy Spirit is NOT the third person of the Godhead" - in our opinion all these are side issues.
